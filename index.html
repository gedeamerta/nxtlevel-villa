About Us

Welcome to Kampung Eropa, a collection of luxurious two floor villas that beautifully blend European elegance with Balinese charm. Nestled in the serene area of Padonan, Canggu, each villa is named after iconic European cities such as Rome, Paris, Barcelona, Amsterdam, and Berlin, showcasing a unique fusion of sophistication and cultural richness.

Just a five minute drive from the stunning beaches of Canggu, Kampung Eropa serves as the perfect base for relaxation, surfing, and exploring nearby attractions. Enjoy access to work, dining, and fitness facilities while savoring the spacious interiors and luxury amenities that each villa offers.

Step outside to discover your private oasis, complete with a shimmering pool and lush tropical gardens that embody the natural beauty of Bali. Here, European refinement meets the tranquility of Balinese living, creating a truly unforgettable experience.

What's Around

Beaches (5-10 minutes)

Explore Bali’s famous beaches like Echo Beach and Batu Bolong, perfect for surfing, sunsets, and beachside dining.

Restaurants & Cafes (3-5 minutes)

A variety of trendy cafes, world- class restaurants, and local eateries are just a stone’s throw away, offering everything from Balinese cuisine to international delicacies.

Fitness & Yoga Studios (5 minutes)

Keep active at nearby fitness centers and yoga studios offering classes and wellness experiences to relax and rejuvenate.

Enjoy the laid-back yet lively nightlife scene with beach bars, live music, and sunset cocktails, all within reach.
